Goals and Objectives

      The vision of Mozilla Open Leaders is to strengthen open projects and communities around the world. Open Leaders will learn to design and build projects that empower others to collaborate within inclusive communities.

  1. Define and demonstrate the terms openness, open leadership and healthier AI.

  2. Design

    1. Illustrate the need for a project, its vision, and its goals

    2. Embrace and communicate the benefits of openness and how to strategically apply different open practices to their work


  3. Build

    1. Setup a project repository on GitHub using best practices for enabling collaboration

      1. Choose and apply open licenses appropriately

    2. Identify and describe multiple pathways for contributors within their work

  4. Empower

    1. Include a broad range of contributors in their work

    2. Create and enforce a safe environment for participation

    3. Communicate their work and vision in a 2min demo or elevator pitch

    4. Promote the values of openness and open leadership to empower others to lead and collaborate

  5. Learn to apply these principles to the AI Project Cycle and Lead an open project.

  6. We aim to help young leaders to think about the people behind the data and build to humanize the stories behind the numbers through these Open Leadership Principles.
